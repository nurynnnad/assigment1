What is BMI?

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a numerical value calculated from a person's height and weight. It is used to assess whether an individual has a healthy body weight for their height.

A BMI score typically falls into categories: underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. It is commonly used to evaluate health risks related to weight but does not account for factors like muscle mass or distribution of fat.
